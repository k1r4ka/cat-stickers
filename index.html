care and maintenance
of your stickers

  • store your stickers in a dry and cool place to avoid damage from moisture and sunlight.

  • avoid sticking stickers on dirty or greasy surfaces, as this can prevent them from adhering well.

  • when applying stickers, make sure the surface is dry and clean. avoid using too hot water or aggressive chemical cleaners.

  • if a sticker gets scratched or wrinkled, you can try applying a small amount of transparent nylon adhesive or nail polish to fix the defect.

  • remember that stickers can be delicate and require careful handling, so keep an eye on them to avoid damage.
